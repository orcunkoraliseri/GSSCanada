# Table 4.1.1: Residential Occupancy Metrics by Period

| Metric | Default | 2005 | 2015 | 2025 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Weekday occupied hours (h) | 16.4 | 15.8 | 17.6 | 11.2 |
| Weekend occupied hours (h) | 16.4 | 13.9 | 15.6 | 12.0 |
| Morning departure time | 08:00 | 10:00 | 08:00 | 08:00 |
| Evening return time | 18:00 | 17:00 | 17:00 | 17:00 |
| Daytime vacancy (09–17), % | 74.4 | 40.3 | 40.0 | 72.4 |

> **Notes**:
> - **Default** is based on DOE MidRise Apartment standard schedule.
> - **Morning Departure / Evening Return** are estimated based on the hour of steepest change in the mean weekday profile.
> - **Daytime Vacancy** is calculated for the 09:00–17:00 period on weekdays.
