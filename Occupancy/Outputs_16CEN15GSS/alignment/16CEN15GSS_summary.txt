COMPARISON WITH 06CEN05GSS
================================================================================

The 06CEN05GSS alignment used these GSS 2005 columns:
  - AGEGR10, sex, marstat, HSDSIZEC, ACT7DAYS
  - SOC91C10, INCM, LUC_RST, PRV, LANHSDC, EDUSTAT

Key differences in GSS 2015:
  1. NOCS: SOC91C10 (GSS2005) -> NOC1110Y (GSS2015) different classification
  2. KOL:  LANHSDC (GSS2005) -> LAN_01 (GSS2015) different variable
  3. ATTSCH: EDUSTAT (GSS2005) -> EDM_02 (GSS2015) different variable
  4. TOTINC: INCM (GSS2005) -> INCG1 (GSS2015) different groupings
